\mainhead{Executive Summary} Silvestre Pambo Bueia is an Angolan rope access professional with experience in maintenance, inspection, and industrial surface preparation/painting. He has worked in oil \& gas environments and related industrial sites, including roles as roustabout and painter, and has also held aviation and travel operations positions as a check-in agent/dispatcher. His training includes IRATA Level 1 rope access and multiple offshore/HSE courses such as HUET, H2S/SCBA, confined space entry, working at height, and fire watch. He is safety-minded and adaptable, with the ability to work in teams and learn quickly. He also has basic computer skills (MS Office) and holds a passport, seaman’s book, and driving license.  \par 
\vspace{8pt} 
\mainhead{Key Achievements} 
\begin{itemizea}  \item \textbf{Rope access and offshore safety readiness:} Completed IRATA Level 1 and key offshore/HSE trainings including HUET, H2S/SCBA, personal survival techniques, confined space entry, working at height, and multigas detector training.
\item \textbf{Industrial maintenance and coating exposure:} Gained hands-on experience in rope access maintenance/inspection and in industrial blast and paint activities.
\item \textbf{Oil \& gas field operations experience:} Worked as a roustabout for Plus Petróleo on land rigs in São Vicente, Cabinda, supporting field operations.
\item \textbf{Aviation and travel operations support:} Performed check-in and dispatcher duties for Fly-540 and for Paccitur (tourism and travel agency).  
\end{itemizea}
