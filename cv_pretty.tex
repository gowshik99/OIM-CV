\begin{paracol}{2}  
\mainhead{Executive Summary} Welding and fabrication professional with extensive experience as a Quality Inspector and Welder across ship repair, mining, and industrial projects in Namibia. Skilled in welding inspection activities including fit-up checks, witnessing NDT, reviewing inspection reports and procedures, and compiling vessel/project data packs. Experienced in developing quality control plans (QCPs), setting hold points, and coordinating with contractors, subcontractors, and third-party inspection providers. Safety-focused with training in SAMTRAC, confined space, and offshore survival (BOSIET), and comfortable working in demanding site and offshore environments. Known for proactive communication, strong work ethic, and reliable execution of quality and repair scopes.  \par 
\vspace{8pt} 
\mainhead{Key Achievements} 
\begin{itemizea}  \item \textbf{Ship repair quality oversight:} Oversaw general quality on ship repair projects at Namdock, including plate/pipe fit-up checks, witnessing NDT on welds, and compiling project data packs per vessel.
\item \textbf{Industrial QA/QC coordination:} Coordinated quality activities with contractors/subcontractors and third-party NDT providers at Swakop Uranium, including audits and review of inspection reports and NDT procedures.
\item \textbf{Testing and qualification witnessing:} Witnessed artisan code qualifications and testing activities, and supported witnessing of hydrostatic and pressure testing as part of project quality control.
\item \textbf{Offshore maintenance welding support:} Executed multi-skilled welding and deck-related repair tasks offshore on DIEP SE MIRA, including removal of sea fastenings/doublers and welding deck floor repairs.  
\end{itemizea}
