\mainhead{Executive Summary} IRATA Level 3 Rope Access professional with experience since 2003 across onshore construction sites, offshore platforms and FPSOs in Angola and South Africa. Skilled in rope access operations, rescue, rigging support, and maintenance assistance for mechanical, instrumentation, electrical and marine teams working at height. Background includes blasting and painting, surface preparation, and inspection-assistant duties on industrial and offshore assets. Experienced as an assistant trainer and rope access trainer/instructor, supporting and leading training activities to IRATA standards. Safety-focused and effective in multinational teams, with strong language capabilities including English and Portuguese.  \par 
\vspace{8pt} 
\mainhead{Key Achievements} 
\begin{itemizea}  \item \textbf{IRATA Level 3 qualification and revalidation:} Completed IRATA Level 3 assessment on 13/12/2013 and successfully revalidated via IRATA L3 refresh on 07/01/2017.
\item \textbf{Rope access training delivery:} Worked as an assistant to a certified IRATA trainer for 2 years and then progressed to lead trainer/instructor roles delivering rope access training.
\item \textbf{Offshore and FPSO project exposure:} Supported maintenance and inspection-related rope access work on multiple Angola assets including Kizomba FPSO, Girasool FPSO, BP PSVM and Pazflor.
\item \textbf{Multi-discipline at-height support:} Assisted production operations (opening/closing valves at height) and supported mechanical, instrumentation, electrical and marine departments for at-height tasks.  
\end{itemizea}
