\begin{paracol}{2}  
\mainhead{Executive Summary} NDT Piping Inspector and IRATA Level 2 Rope Access Technician with offshore oil \& gas experience in Angola, supporting inspection and maintenance activities on FPSOs and terminals. Performs external visual inspections of piping and pressure equipment, assesses coating condition and corrosion, and takes UT wall thickness measurements when required. Experienced in extracting and using piping isometrics and P\&IDs to trace lines on topsides and execute inspection scopes, including GRP pre-inspection campaigns. Conducts post-weld repair controls using PT and MT methods in workshop and on site. Recognized as a proactive, reliable professional with a strong safety mindset and ability to support multi-discipline teams at height.  \par 
\vspace{8pt} 
\mainhead{Key Achievements} 
\begin{itemizea}  \item \textbf{Offshore inspection assignments on multiple FPSOs:} Delivered rope-access supported inspection work on FPSO Girassol, FPSO Dalia and FPSO Clov for Total E\&P Angola (Block 17).
\item \textbf{GRP pre-inspection and line status classification:} Performed line inspections to determine FFLB/NFFLB status (Fit/Not Fit for Live Blasting) and reported findings for superintendent validation during GRP campaign pre-inspections.
\item \textbf{Pressure vessel inspection support:} Executed close visual inspections of pressure vessels (external and internal) and structural elements according to client scope of work.
\item \textbf{Post-repair NDT controls:} Performed controls after welding repairs using PT and MT methods in workshop and on site.  
\end{itemizea}
