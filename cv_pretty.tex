\mainhead{Executive Summary} Angolan welder with extensive offshore and industrial project experience across Angola, including major oil \& gas developments. Trained in multiple welding processes (MIG/MAG, SMAW 6G/6GR, SAW and TIG on carbon steel) and supporting activities such as oxy-acetylene cutting and gouging. Has worked on fabrication yard and barge-based scopes, including deck/manifold and pile/spooling/jumper works on large projects. Holds safety and offshore-related training including H2S, confined space entry, personal survival/water survival techniques and firewatcher. Recognized for leadership capability, having served as a team leader for several years while maintaining a safety-focused approach.  \par 
\vspace{8pt} 
\mainhead{Key Achievements} 
\begin{itemizea}  \item \textbf{Multi-process welding capability:} Completed training and applied multiple welding processes including MIG/MAG, SMAW (6G/6GR), SAW and TIG (carbon steel) across offshore and yard environments.
\item \textbf{Major Angola oil \& gas project exposure:} Contributed to projects including Block 31 PSVM (BP), Mafumeira (Chevron) and Kaombo (Total) in roles under Heerema and other employers.
\item \textbf{Team leadership experience:} Worked as a team leader for four years, coordinating welding activities and supporting team execution on site.
\item \textbf{Offshore safety readiness:} Completed H2S basic training, confined space entry and personal/water survival techniques, and served as Firewatcher on Transocean Skyros (Block 32).
