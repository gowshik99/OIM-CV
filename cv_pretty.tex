\mainhead{Executive Summary} IRATA Level 2 rope access technician with extensive experience in offshore and industrial environments, including FPSO vessels, shipyards, power stations, and commercial buildings. Skilled in surface preparation, blasting and painting, pressure washing, and structural touch-up work, as well as rigging support tasks such as bolt torquing, chain blocks, lifting/lowering, and pulley systems. Has performed maintenance and access work at height and overboard, including rescue standby duties and rope-to-rope transfers. Safety-focused with training in offshore survival, confined space, first aid, and HSE, and accustomed to working on international assignments across Angola, UAE, Ghana, and Gabon. Brings a hands-on approach that combines practical execution with disciplined adherence to procedures and client requirements.  \par

\begin{itemizea}  \item \textbf{Offshore FPSO maintenance assignments:} Completed multiple rope-access blasting, painting, and touch-up scopes on FPSO Girassol (Block 17, Angola) across several contract periods with Ropetec/Prezioso.
\item \textbf{International rope access projects (UAE):} Delivered rope-access maintenance and surface preparation work across multiple Dubai/Abu Dhabi sites, including Drydock World and power station locations, covering blasting, painting, netting, and mechanical prep support.

\item \textbf{Rigging and valve change support (West Africa):} Performed bolt torquing, chain block rigging, lifting/lowering, and valve change support on FPSO-related assignments in Ghana and Gabon with Axess Africa/Yinson.
