\begin{paracol}{2}  
\mainhead{Executive Summary} Rope access maintenance technician with offshore experience in Angola, supporting FPSO operations across multiple Total E\&P assets. Background includes storekeeping/stock control, blasting and painting, and rope-access-based maintenance activities on piping and structures. Experienced in general maintenance support, fire and gas equipment maintenance, rigging support, bolt torqueing/preservation, and assisting multiple service providers with installations on board. Trained to IRATA Level 1 and Level 2 and familiar with offshore safety requirements including STCW95 modules. Recognized as proactive, reliable, and safety-minded with the ability to learn multi-discipline techniques.  \par 
\vspace{8pt} 
\mainhead{Key Achievements} 
\begin{itemizea}  \item \textbf{Offshore stock control support (Block 17):} Supported offshore logistics as a storeman/stock controller on Block 17, contributing to materials availability for operations on the African Worker.
\item \textbf{Protective coating work on major FPSOs:} Performed blasting and painting to protect asset integrity on DALIA, GIRASSOL, PAZFLOR and other offshore installations for Total E\&P Angola.
\item \textbf{Core rope access maintenance team (Greater Plutonio):} Served as core crew rope access maintenance team member on FPSO Greater Plutonio, supporting maintenance, equipment installation assistance, and rescue readiness.  
\end{itemizea}  
\vspace{8pt}

\end{itemize}
