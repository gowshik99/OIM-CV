\mainhead{Executive Summary} Rope Access Technician and industrial spray painter with experience in offshore and industrial environments in Angola. Skilled in surface preparation and coating activities including blasting, UHP water jetting, and manual preparation prior to painting. Experienced in painting pipe systems, equipment, and structures, and in installing rigging equipment to support maintenance work at height. Has worked on maintenance and revamping activities, including GRP-related work and FPSO maintenance exposure. Safety- and maintenance-focused, able to support teams on demanding sites and adapt to dynamic work environments.  \par

\mainhead{Key Achievements} 
\begin{itemizea}  \item \textbf{Offshore industrial exposure:} Worked across multiple offshore blocks (17, 2, 31, 14, 15) and bases/sites including Malongo base, Kwanda, Petromar (Ambriz) and Painal.
\item \textbf{Surface preparation \& coating delivery:} Performed end-to-end surface preparation (blasting, UHP, manual prep) and industrial painting on pipe systems, equipment and structures.
\item \textbf{Work-at-height maintenance support:} Installed rigging equipment and assisted maintenance teams to execute jobs at height using rope access methods.
\item \textbf{Project participation:} Contributed to GRP project activities and revamping/maintenance work on Girassol FPSO.  
\end{itemizea}
