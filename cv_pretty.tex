\begin{paracol}{2}  
\mainhead{Executive Summary} IRATA-certified Rope Access Level 2 technician with nearly seven years of offshore experience performing painting, blasting and maintenance activities at height. Worked across multiple FPSOs and offshore assets, including Girassol FPSO and Kizomba B (Block 15) as well as Block 31 projects with BP. Strong background in surface preparation and coating systems for gas pipelines, including mechanical preparation to ST2/ST3 standards and application of primer, intermediate and top coats. Experienced in overboard rope-access touch-up using spray equipment and in confined-space blasting in ballast tanks. Safety- and quality-focused, able to support rigging/chain block installation and basic electrical installation tasks when required.  \par 
\vspace{8pt} 
\mainhead{Key Achievements} 
\begin{itemizea}  \item \textbf{Offshore FPSO rope-access painting and blasting scope:} Delivered rope-access painting and blasting work on offshore gas pipelines and structures across several FPSOs (including Girassol FPSO and Kizomba B), covering surface preparation, coating application and touch-up activities.
\item \textbf{Surface preparation to required standards:} Performed mechanical surface preparation using MBX, chipping hammer, sanding machine and needle gun to meet ST2/ST3 quality requirements prior to coating.
\item \textbf{Coating system application:} Applied primer, intermediate and top coats using products such as Carbomastic, Carboguard 893 and Carbothane (Part A \& B), including masking and thinner use for cleaning/mixing.
\item \textbf{Blasting operations including confined spaces:} Executed blasting activities in ballast tanks (confined spaces) and on ropes using blasting machines, compressors and accessories, working to SA1, SA2, SA2.5 and SA3 blasting qualities.  
\end{itemizea}  
\vspace{8pt} 
\mainhead{Professional Experience} 
\role{Rope Access Technician / Painter / Blaster (IRATA L2)}{Girassol FPSO / Kizomba B (KizB) FPSO, Block 15; Block 31 (BP) FPSO}{2013 to 2019}
\begin{itemize}
\item Performed rope-access painting and blasting offshore, including overboard touch-up using spray gun and confined-space blasting in ballast tanks.
\item Prepared pipe surfaces prior to painting using mechanical tools (MBX, chipping hammer, sanding machine, needle gun) to ST2/ST3 standards; applied primer, intermediate and top coats with masking as required.
\item Supported maintenance tasks such as chain-block/slings installation and assisted with basic electrical works (junction boxes, cable dressing, lighting installation, cable pulling).
\end{itemize}
