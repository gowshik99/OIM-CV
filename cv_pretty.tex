\mainhead{Executive Summary} QC Welding and Fabrication Inspector with extensive experience across metal fabrication, ship repair, offshore and petrochemical pipeline environments in Namibia and South Africa. Skilled in dimensional and visual inspection against drawings, specifications and material standards, with strong focus on final product acceptance and client sign-off. Experienced in compiling Quality Data Books and supporting quality systems and audits, including work aligned to DNV and ISO standards. Competent in developing welding documentation such as WPS/PQR and supporting welder qualifications (WQR). Holds Level 1 inspection certifications (SAIW Welding \& Fabrication Inspector, PT and MT) and offshore safety/medical training, with a safety-minded and reliable approach in demanding industrial settings.  \par 
\vspace{8pt} 
\mainhead{Key Achievements} 
\begin{itemizea}  \item \textbf{DNV and ISO-aligned quality work:} Performed inspection and sign-off activities on DNV cages to DNV 2.7-1 and 2.7-3 standards and supported audits aligned to ISO 45001/9001 and ISO 17020 while at 3C Metal Belmet.
\item \textbf{Major industrial and offshore projects exposure:} Contributed QC inspection support across multiple high-profile projects and clients including De Beers AMV3/ADS (Benguella Gem), offshore drill ships and service providers (e.g., Schlumberger, Oceaneering, Seadril, Valaris, Odfjell Drilling, Baker Hughes).
\item \textbf{Welding procedure documentation:} Developed and supported welding procedure and qualification documentation including WPS/PQR and WQR as part of QC Welding and Fabricating Inspector duties at RITC Namibia.
\item \textbf{Uranium sector fabrication and refurbishment:} Supported QC inspection for Rossing Uranium new acid leach tanks construction and Husab Uranium refurbishment of hauling truck bins during tenure at NEC Stahl Namibia.  
\end{itemizea}
