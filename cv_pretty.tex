\mainhead{Executive Summary} IRATA Level 3 Rope Access Supervisor and NDT Assistant with offshore experience on FPSOs and platforms in Angola blocks 3, 15 and 17. Skilled in planning and coordinating rope access operations, conducting task-based risk assessments, and supervising inspection teams during piping campaigns and structural integrity surveys. Hands-on exposure to NDT support activities including eddy current testing and phased array ultrasonic testing (PAUT) for corrosion mapping and riser monitoring, with strong focus on reporting and interpretation of results. Experienced in maintenance and inspection of critical offshore equipment such as free-fall lifeboat davits, cranes, and hydraulic systems. Safety-focused professional trained in offshore survival, confined space, H2S, firefighting, and first aid, accustomed to working in high-risk environments and supporting client demonstrations onshore and offshore.  \par 
\vspace{8pt} 
\mainhead{Key Achievements} 
\begin{itemizea}  \item \textbf{Free-fall davit maintenance \& load testing support:} Supported free-fall davit maintenance and load testing campaigns, including pre- and post-load test inspections using eddy current and compilation of internal NDT reporting and post-project reviews.
\item \textbf{PAUT piping campaign leadership (Block 3):} Led rope access/inspection teams during Block 3 piping campaign surveys using PAUT with corrosion mapping, including client demonstrations and development of a PAUT reporting template for the campaign.
\item \textbf{Crane inspection reporting:} Performed rope-access-enabled crane inspections using eddy current and compiled reports with findings and interpretation of test results.
\item \textbf{Riser monitoring inspections:} Performed routine inspections for riser monitoring onboard using phased array equipment and produced routine inspection reports.  
\end{itemizea}
