\mainhead{Executive Summary} NDT Level 2 Inspector and rope access technician with offshore and onshore experience in Angola’s Oil \& Gas sector, supporting inspection, quality control and maintenance activities on FPSOs. Experienced performing routine NDT and QA/QC on piping networks, pressure vessels, storage tanks, welded equipment and structural components, with exposure to corrosion monitoring and cathodic protection checks. Comfortable working under permit-to-work systems, participating in safety meetings and producing inspection reports using Excel and site systems such as SAP. Selected to support inspection campaigns including radiography (X-ray), eddy current, drone inspection coordination with third parties and flexible hose inspections. Safety-focused and team-oriented, with experience assisting and guiding junior technicians and contributing to emergency response readiness offshore.  \par

\mainhead{Key Achievements} 
\begin{itemizea}  \item \textbf{Offshore FPSO inspection delivery:} Performed routine NDT and QA/QC inspections across multiple FPSOs (CLOV, PAZFLOR, GIRASOL, KIZOMBA B) covering piping, tanks, pressure vessels, welded equipment and structural elements.
\item \textbf{Inspection campaigns support:} Chosen to support and drive inspection campaigns such as X-ray/radiography, eddy current, flexible hose inspections and coordination of drone inspections with third-party providers.
\item \textbf{Permit-to-work and reporting:} Created permits to work and produced inspection reporting using Excel and site tools including ADHOC and SAP.
\item \textbf{Emergency response participation:} Participated as part of an FPSO emergency response team, strengthening discipline and posture during emergency situations.  
\end{itemizea}
