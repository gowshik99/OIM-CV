\mainhead{Executive Summary} Offshore maintenance professional with extensive experience on FPSOs in Angola, specializing in rope access, rigging, scaffolding, and confined space rescue. Has worked on long-term maintenance assignments on Pazflor FPSO (since 2012) and previously on Dalia FPSO (2009–2012), supporting multiple onboard disciplines with work-at-height assistance and rigging equipment installations. Experienced member of medical/ERP and rope access rescue teams, with strong safety focus in offshore environments. Background also includes grit blasting and scaffolding work with Prezioso Angola (2006–2009) and assignments across several vessels and projects. Holds IRATA Level 3 and multiple offshore safety and access certifications enabling autonomous execution and supervision of high-risk tasks.  \par
