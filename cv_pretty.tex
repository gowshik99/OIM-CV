\mainhead{Executive Summary} Antonio Garcia is a Rope Access Technician (IRATA Level 2) with multi-method NDT Level 2 qualifications (UT, UTM, PT and MT) and lifting gear inspection training. He has experience supporting offshore/industrial inspection and maintenance activities, including surface preparation, painting and blasting work performed at height and in confined environments. His background also includes HVAC technician work and earlier roles in shipping as a cadet engineer, providing exposure to marine operations and safety requirements. He is trained in key maritime safety modules (HUET, BST, survival at sea, fire prevention and first aid) and is familiar with tanker operations. He is able to work in safety-critical environments and follow procedures, documentation and inspection standards.  \par 
\vspace{8pt} 
\mainhead{Key Achievements} 
\begin{itemizea}  \item \textbf{Multi-method NDT Level 2 qualification:} Certified to SNT-TC-1A Level 2 in UT, UTM, PT and MT, enabling inspection support across multiple NDT methods.
\item \textbf{Rope access capability:} IRATA Rope Access Level 2 qualified for access and maintenance/inspection tasks at height.
\item \textbf{Lifting and bolting competence:} Completed Lifting Gear Inspection (CERTEX) and bolting specialist training to support mechanical integrity activities.
\item \textbf{Maritime safety training:} Completed maritime safety courses including HUET, BST, survival at sea, fire prevention and fighting, and elementary first aid.
