\begin{paracol}{2}  
\mainhead{Executive Summary} Industrial and rig welder with extensive offshore and shipyard experience supporting drilling and FPSO projects in Angola and West Africa. Skilled in fabrication, repair and preventive maintenance of metal structures across rig areas such as rig floor, moon pool, pipe/riser decks, engine room and subsea support zones. Qualified in multiple welding processes and positions including 6G/6GR with SMAW, GMAW (MIG/MAG), FCAW and GTAW, and experienced with cutting and plasma operations. IRATA Level 1 Rope Access Technician with a strong safety mindset and ability to work under pressure in multicultural teams. Known for reliability, punctuality and proactive support across departments during maintenance and modification scopes.  \par 
\vspace{8pt} 
\mainhead{Key Achievements} 
\begin{itemizea}  \item \textbf{Offshore drillship maintenance support (West Gemini):} Executed cutting and modification tasks (including shoes and padeyes on 36" casings) and supported maintenance activities across multiple departments (rig floor, moon pool, pipe deck, riser deck, engine room and subsea).
\item \textbf{Multi-block rig welding assignments (West Polaris):} Delivered manufacture/repair of metal structures and preventive maintenance support during successive campaigns in Block 16 (Maersk), Block 15 (ESSO) and Equatorial Guinea (ESSO).
\item \textbf{Major terminal rehabilitation and tank construction:} Contributed to rehabilitation/expansion works at Luanda maritime terminal (TEMAR) and other Sonangol terminals, including construction of multiple storage tanks and associated piping.
\item \textbf{FPSO module and spool fabrication (Sonamet):} Participated in fabrication activities for FPSO-related modules and components (including flare boom, mooring buoy and spools) across multiple oil \& gas projects.  
\end{itemizea}
