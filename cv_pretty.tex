\begin{paracol}{2}  
\mainhead{Executive Summary} Rope access and offshore maintenance professional with 8+ years of experience supporting TOTAL E\&P Angola on FPSO assets including Pazflor, Dalia and Girassol. Experienced in surface preparation, coating application and maintenance activities using specialized tools and working to required surface preparation standards (e.g., ST2/ST3). Has supported equipment removal/replacement tasks using rope access techniques and rigging/chain blocks, and has assisted inspection activities such as coating tests. Trained in key offshore safety requirements including permit to work, confined space entry and PPE standards. Known for flexibility, readiness to mobilize and ability to work effectively in teams in demanding offshore environments.  \par 
\vspace{8pt} 
\mainhead{Key Achievements} 
\begin{itemizea}  \item \textbf{FPSO Pazflor surface repair and maintenance:} Performed surface repair and maintenance on FPSO PAZ-FLOR using MBX, chipping hammer, sanding machine and needle gun, preparing surfaces to required ST2/ST3 quality and applying protective paint.
\item \textbf{Dalia FPSO equipment replacement support:} Supported removal and replacement of equipment (spools/valves) on DALIA FPSO using rope access techniques and chain blocks/rigging to lift and reinstall components.
\item \textbf{Pazflor painting and inspection assistance:} Maintained pipe spools and structures with protective coatings on PAZ-FLOR and assisted inspection activities including crane coating tests on SKIROS (Block 32).
\item \textbf{Girassol revamping project contribution:} Worked on the Girassol Revamping Project as maintenance technician and as blaster/painter, including work in water ballast tanks and on piping networks.  
\end{itemizea}
