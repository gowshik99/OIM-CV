\mainhead{Executive Summary} Welding Quality Inspector with experience in industrial fabrication and terminal operations, progressing from welder to team leader and then QA/QC inspection roles. Skilled in monitoring welding activities before, during and after execution, coordinating NDT requests, and issuing final inspection reports. Background includes hands-on welding processes (GTAW, SMAW, FCAW) and leadership of welding teams with focus on HSSE and quality compliance. Also experienced as a fire fighter performing routine inspections of fire-fighting equipment and identifying fire-risk areas. Recognized for clear communication, teamwork, time management and a safety-focused mindset in site environments.  \par

\begin{itemizea}  \item \textbf{Progression to QA/QC inspection roles:} Advanced from Welder (2008–2012) to Welding Team Leader (2012–2015) and Quality Inspector (2015–2020), culminating in Welding Quality Inspector role since April 2022.
\item \textbf{Welding inspection and reporting:} Performed welding process evaluation before, during and after welding, coordinated NDT requests, and produced final inspection reports.
\item \textbf{Team supervision and HSSE compliance:} Led welding teams by distributing tasks, supervising execution, ensuring HSSE and quality rules were followed, and reporting completed work for visual inspection.
\item \textbf{Fire safety inspections:} Conducted regular inspections of fire-fighting devices and assessed work areas for potential fire risks as part of fire-fighting duties.
