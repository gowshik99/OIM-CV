\mainhead{Executive Summary} Industrial Maintenance Technician and IRATA Level 2 Rope Access Technician with over 6 years of experience in the oil and gas industry, delivering general maintenance, inspection support, and corrosion-control activities offshore and onshore in Angola. Experienced on FPSO environments (CLOV, GIRASSOL, DALIA) performing work at height and in confined spaces, including bolting, rigging/mooring for lifting operations, and mechanical support tasks around pumps, valves, and turbo generator installations. Strong safety mindset with routine participation in daily/weekly safety meetings and experience supporting confined space entry control and rescue readiness. Comfortable working in multicultural teams and client-facing environments, communicating daily work needs and progress. Available for offshore rotations (28/28) and adaptable to demanding schedules and conditions.  \par 
\vspace{8pt} 
\mainhead{Key Achievements} 
\begin{itemizea}  \item \textbf{FPSO CLOV (Total Angola Block 17) support:} Supported offshore maintenance and lifting activities as IRATA Level 2 technician and rigger, including pump bolting, pneumatic valve operations, turbo generator installation support, and corrosion-control work on hot piping.
\item \textbf{Confined space safety role:} Served as confined space entry attendant/security guard, focusing on risk identification, control measures, and timely communication to personnel involved in the entry.
\item \textbf{Team leadership support:} Acted as assistant supervisor/team leader, reinforcing organization, preparation, punctuality, and teamwork during offshore operations.
\item \textbf{Multi-FPSO project experience:} Delivered rope access, painting/decapage, and maintenance support across multiple FPSOs (GIRASSOL GRP project and DALIA pull-cables project) with consistent participation in safety meetings.  
\end{itemizea}
