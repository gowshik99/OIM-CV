\mainhead{Executive Summary} Electrician and Maintenance Technician with experience in onshore and offshore oil \& gas environments in Angola. Worked on electrical installation and maintenance of security systems and electrical appliances, as well as mechanical support activities including hydrostatic/hydrotesting and hydraulic bolt tensioning and torqueing operations. Experienced in assembling, installing, testing, and maintaining equipment, troubleshooting anomalies, and performing preventive and corrective interventions.
