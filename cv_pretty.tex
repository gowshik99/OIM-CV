\mainhead{Executive Summary} Osvaldo Vicente Mangovo is an Angolan Rope Access Technician (IRATA Level 1) and NDT Inspector qualified in PT and MT Level 2, with extensive offshore and base experience in oil \& gas blocks in Angola. He has worked as a freelancer and contractor across inspection, maintenance, rigging/assembly, and piping disciplines on major projects in Blocks 15 and 17 (e.g., Girassol, Kizomba). His background includes pile inspection support, maintenance rope access activities, and pipefitting work on washing/PIG post and satellite projects. He is trained in offshore safety and emergency response (BST, HUET, H2O/SCBA, Total HSE) and operates with a strong safety mindset. He is recognized for adaptability, punctuality, and effective communication in multicultural site environments.  \par
