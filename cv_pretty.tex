\mainhead{Executive Summary} Mechanical Technician and Turner Machinist with extensive offshore and marine maintenance experience, including riser installation (hook-up), commissioning, and shipyard/dry-dock work. Skilled in inspection, repair and maintenance of drilling risers and associated equipment, with hands-on exposure to pressure testing and mechanical tasks in offshore environments. NDT qualified (PT, MPI and Ultrasonic Wall Thickness) with experience applying PT/MPI on riser-related components. Background includes crane/forklift operations, rigging and slinging, and maintaining safety equipment, with a strong safety mindset. Proven ability to work in multidisciplinary teams offshore and on vessels, supporting operations from installation through first oil/start-up.  \par

\begin{itemizea}  \item \textbf{NDT qualification progression (2023–2024):} Obtained NDT certifications including Liquid Penetrant Testing (L2), Magnetic Particle Testing (L2) and Ultrasonic Wall Thickness (L2), building on earlier LPI/MPI training with Global Riser.
\item \textbf{Offshore FLNG turret/riser installation support:} Supported riser/hook-up and turret installation activities offshore Mozambique on the Coral Sul FLNG as a Mechanical Technician.
\item \textbf{FPSO hook-up to first oil support:} Performed winch operations and mechanical support for riser installation (hook-up), commissioning, modifications and maintenance through first oil/start-up onboard FPSO Abigail Joseph offshore Nigeria.
\item \textbf{Long-term progression in marine/industrial operations:} Held multiple roles at Namdeb Diamond Corporation from fitter/turner and deckhand through safety representative and mechanical projects foreman, demonstrating progression across operations, safety and project execution.
