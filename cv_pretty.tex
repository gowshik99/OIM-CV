\mainhead{Executive Summary} Rope Access and Level II NDT Inspector with around five years of oil and gas experience across onshore and offshore projects in Africa. Experienced in UT thickness gauging, VT/CVI, RBI surveys, and multi-method NDT (ET, MPI/MT, PT, PMI, thermography and vibration/condition monitoring) on piping, pressure vessels, tanks and structural components. Skilled in confined space inspections, interpreting P\&IDs/isometric drawings, and compiling inspection and quality documentation to client and code requirements. Has supported major offshore FPSO and LNG assets, balancing operational integrity with strict safety and quality assurance. Recognised for strong defect detection capability, including identifying a critical hull leakage offshore in Angola as part of a two-person team.  \par 
\vspace{8pt} 
\mainhead{Key Achievements} 
\begin{itemizea}  \item \textbf{Critical defect detection offshore:} Part of a two-person team that detected a leakage in a drill ship hull structure offshore Angola; recognised by the company Board of Directors and remunerated accordingly.
\item \textbf{Angola LNG integrity support:} Maintained operational integrity and reliability activities for the Angola LNG facility through multi-method NDT, RBI and condition monitoring support.
\item \textbf{Block 15 FPSO inspection delivery:} Performed rope-access UT pipe inspections, marine inspections, RBI surveys and confined-space tank inspections across Offshore Block 15 FPSO assets (Kizomba A/B/C and Mondo).
\item \textbf{Multi-method NDT execution:} Executed VT/UT/MPI( MT)/PT/ET and CP inspections to identify defects for maintenance or replacement on offshore platforms, rigs and vessels.  
\end{itemizea}
