\begin{paracol}{2}  
\mainhead{Executive Summary} Filisbino Samuel Ucalana is an Angolan offshore maintenance professional with experience in rope access, painting/blasting, and facilities/mechanical support on FPSOs in Angola. He has worked on major offshore assets including Girassol, Dalia, Pazflor and FPSO Greater Plutonio, supporting integrity preservation through protective coatings and general maintenance activities. His background includes fire and gas equipment maintenance support, rigging and lifting assistance, bolt torqueing/preservation, and installation support for service providers requiring rope access. He is trained to IRATA Rope Access Levels 1 and 2 and has completed offshore safety training (STCW 95). He is described as proactive, reliable, flexible and team-oriented, with a strong safety mindset for offshore operations.  \par 
\vspace{8pt} 
\mainhead{Key Achievements} 
\begin{itemizea}  \item \textbf{Offshore asset integrity preservation:} Performed painting and blasting activities to protect piping (flanges, valves, bolts) and structures with protective coatings on multiple TOTAL E\&P Angola FPSOs (Dalia, Girassol, Pazflor).
\item \textbf{Core crew rope access maintenance:} Served as core crew in a rope access maintenance team on FPSO Greater Plutonio (Block 18), providing ongoing maintenance support and rope-access assistance for onboard service providers.
\item \textbf{Safety and emergency response readiness:} Participated in confined space rescue team and the Greater Plutonio rescue team, supporting emergency preparedness alongside maintenance duties.
\item \textbf{Offshore logistics support:} Worked as storeman/stock controller offshore (Block 17), supporting materials control and logistics on the African Worker.  
\end{itemizea}
