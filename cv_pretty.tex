\mainhead{Executive Summary} Rope Access Technician and NDT Level 2 professional with offshore experience supporting inspection, quality control, and maintenance activities on FPSOs and platforms in Angola. Worked across multiple blocks for major operators including ExxonMobil and Sonangol/Tacula, contributing to NDT inspection and QA/QC as well as surface preparation and coating works. Experienced in rope access operations, blasting and painting scopes, and industrial environments requiring strict safety compliance. Holds offshore safety trainings including BST and HUET, with a practical, adaptable approach to field work. Known for being organized, punctual, and able to integrate easily within teams while maintaining confidentiality and professional conduct.  \par 
\vspace{8pt} 
\mainhead{Key Achievements} 
\begin{itemizea}  \item \textbf{Offshore FPSO inspection support (Block 15):} Performed rope access and NDT/quality control activities as a freelancer for ISQAPAVE on ExxonMobil-operated assets including FPSO MONDO, FPSO SAXI Batuque, and Kizomba B.
\item \textbf{Multi-block rope access coating works:} Delivered rope access support for blasting and painting activities with PREZIOSO across Block 14, Block 0 (Tacula/Plataforma Hotel), and Block 3 (Cabinda).
\item \textbf{Tank cleaning operations offshore:} Worked as a tank cleaning operator with NAFCLEAN on Block 0 assets including Plataforma Hotel and Tacula.
\item \textbf{Industrial welding assistance on LNG project:} Supported welding activities as a Welding Assistant with PETROMAR on the Angola LNG project at the Kwanda base in Soyo.  
\end{itemizea}
